Green the Way (Android Game)


Game Green The Way tells the story of a teenager who lives in urban areas, namely Rits. Rits cares so much about his environment that when there is garbage scattered on the streets he always tries to throw it in the trash. In the Green The Way game, there are two modes that the user can choose, namely morning or morning mode and night or night mode. In addition, in this game, there are obstacles in the form of banana traps and other objects that Rits must avoid - which makes the user challenged to pass them. There is also a boost in the form of coins that can make the Rits character run with a high enough acceleration so that it is more difficult for players to avoid obstacles, but is given the convenience so that every time the user jumps he will not fall to the road surface for a few seconds. Then, in this game there is also a Best Score system, which is a system that can calculate the maximum score obtained by the user. Let's help Rits to clean up trash on the streets and make urban streets greener!

Bitrado: Bucin Travelling Indonesia (Android Game)


Bitrado is a game application that combines games and benefits to support MSMEs and local tourism. This application has the advantage of increasing regional MSME sales. In addition, introducing the culture that exists in every region of Indonesia in the national and international arena, and elevating the economy of the people in Indonesia by working online during the Covid-19 pandemic (as a contributor in our game application, namely designers). This game application has missions and challenges to explore Indonesian tourism, such as buying clothes in an area, buying special foods in an area with the integration of MSMEs in Indonesia, and learning the culture of a place, so that later they will get rewards as feedback. . So it is hoped that it can provide a new traveling experience as a form of solution in responding to the economic shortcomings of the tourism sector in Indonesia during the New Normal.

Binjai Invasion (Android Game)


Binjai Invasion tells the story of a student at a university in a city called RAF. He is a teenager who always has the urge to hit a banana tree he sees with the excuse of testing his hand strength. One day, RAF was walking around his university and he saw a huge banana tree garden, but he saw that there were security guards, CCTV and detection lights to secure the banana trees in the garden. In the Binjai Invasion game, the RAF character has a mission to hit the banana tree by passing several obstacles, including security guards, CCTV, and detection lights. The user is asked to think and be able to manage the time that has been determined by making a path so that the RAF character can hit the banana tree that is guarded. In addition, there is an online leaderboard for users at each level, and the online leaderboard will change its ranking position according to the fastest time created by the user. At each level, there is a different level of difficulty and time that makes the user feel challenged. Let's help the RAF to complete the mission of hitting the banana tree!

INCLUDE-C19: (Internet of Things)


INCLUDE-C19 is a smart camera system based on Internet of Things and Machine Learning. This system can automatically detect human objects and provide audible warnings when there is a physical distancing violation and provide a sound warning when a violation occurs without wearing a mask. This system is equipped with a mobile-based application called the INCLUDE App that can be used by officers to control, monitor, and receive notification of violations from this camera system.
