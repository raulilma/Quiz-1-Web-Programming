Pisang Gapit

Pisang Gapit is a typical culinary from the Kalimantan region which is made from bananas. Bananas are a fruit that many people like because of their sweet taste and many benefits for the body.

In Balikpapan

2nd Place

Bubur Gunting

It may sound a bit strange if we hear the term "Bubur Gunting". However, this typical Balikpapan food is indeed processed from glutinous rice flour. In the manufacturing process, the dough from this food will be cut into pieces before being cooked with coconut milk and brown sugar.

3rd Place

Bingka Kentang

The next typical Balikpapan food is "Bingka Kentang". It is called so because one of the ingredients used in this culinary is the main ingredient to use potatoes. With the original sweet taste, now potato frames have also been made in various variants ranging from chocolate, brown sugar, to banana and cheese flavors.
